# <Basics /> Resources

<div className="overview-header">
  <div className="overview-text">
    <p>
        MSDL is an open platform for anyone interested in exploring the natural resources, environment, and culture in and around Mekong River Basin. MSDL brings a variety of Open Access (OA) documentary and media resources to its Readers. However, Readers can also access Resources that will require them to Sign-up in MSDL.
        <br />
        <br />
        <h4>Open Access Resources</h4>              
        Some resources are published as 'Open Access'. MSDL makes such resources available accessible to all the Readers, without requiring them to log in the MSDL Apps.
        <br />
        <br />
        <h4>Restricted Access Resources</h4>
        Document and media resources with license that doesn't allow free and open access through MSDL will require the  that are published for resticted circulation. To read such restriced resources, Readers shall require to Sign-up in MSDL App.
    </p>
