# Journal Article

## Introduction

An academic journal or scholarly journal is a periodical publication in which scholarship relating to a particular
academic discipline is published. An article is long form structured writing published in a journal.

:::tip Expore Journal Articles

To get a list of Journal Articles, select 'Journal Article' as Resource Type in MSDL Explore. You can then use various
filters such as Author, Publisher, and Tags to narrow down the list of these Journal Articles.

<Thumbnail src="/img/journal/explore-journal-article.png" alt="Selecting Journal Article in explore" />

:::

## Journal Article Details {#journal-article-details}

A Journal Article is cited like this:

```
Author’s Last Name, Initial(s)., Author’s Last Name, Initial(s)., & Author’s Last Name, Initial(s). (Year of
publication). Title of article. Title of Journal, Volume(Issue), Pages. https://doi.org/DOI or URL
```

You can read in Web, Android, or iOS App.
