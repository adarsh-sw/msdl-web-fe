# What is the Mekong School Digital Library?

<div className="gallery">
  <div className="sub-heading">
    <div className="front-matter">
      <p>
        <Link to="https://library.mekongschool.org" target="_blank">
          <b>The Mekong School Digital Library (MSDL)</b>
        </Link>{' '}
        is an online library of documents, media, and data on the natural resources, environment, and culture in and
        around the Mekong River Basin.
        <br />
        <br />
        Implemented by The Asia Foundation and run by the Mekong School, MSDL is an initiative of the USAID and Australia
        Mekong Safeguards Program.
        <br />
        <br />
        Launched in May 2023, MSDL users comprise of students, educators, youth, researchers, practitioners, public
        officers, and community members.
        <br />
        <br />A wide variety of documentary and media resources is available in MSDL. Documentary resources are Books,
        Blogs, Dissertation & Thesis, Essay & Article, Interview, Journal Articles, Legal Texts, Magazine Articles, Map,
        News Article, Policy Briefing, Presentation, Proceedings, and Reports.
        <br />
        <br />
        Media resources in MSDL include Audio & Podcast, Image, and Video.
